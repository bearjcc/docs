We want every remote employee to have a workspace that lets them do their best work. To make that happen, Autex provides a new hire stipend, company-issued equipment, monthly internet reimbursement, and ergonomic support. This page walks you through each benefit and how to access it.

## New hire home office stipend

Full-time remote employees receive a one-time **$XXX home office stipend** to help you get your workspace set up. You can use this stipend from your first day, and you have **90 days from your start date** to submit receipts through the expense tool.

<Tip>
  Submit your receipts as you purchase items rather than waiting until day 89 — it keeps things simple and ensures you don't miss the window.
</Tip>

For full instructions on submitting expenses, see [Expense Reimbursement](/policies/expense-reimbursement).

### What's covered

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Covered ✅" icon="circle-check">
    - Monitor, keyboard, mouse, and peripherals
    - Desk, chair, and ergonomic furniture
    - Lighting and webcam for video calls
    - Headset or headphones
  </Card>
  <Card title="Not covered ❌" icon="circle-xmark">
    - General household items not primarily used for work
    - Upgrades to equipment already provided by the company
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

<Note>
  If you're unsure whether a purchase is covered, ask HR before buying. It's much easier to get clarity upfront than to dispute a reimbursement later.
</Note>

## Company-provided equipment

IT ships your laptop before your start date — you don't need to do anything to request it. If you need additional hardware beyond the laptop (such as a monitor, docking station, or other peripherals), you can request it after you start.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Submit an IT ticket">
    Open a ticket at [IT Access Requests](/it-security/access-requests) and specify the equipment you need and a brief justification.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Get manager approval">
    Additional equipment requests require your manager's approval. Loop them in on the ticket or ask them to add a comment confirming the request.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Wait for shipment">
    IT will confirm and ship approved items. Delivery timelines vary — reach out to IT directly if you have an urgent need.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Internet reimbursement

Autex reimburses up to **$XX per month** toward your home internet bill.

- **How to submit:** Use the expense tool, select the **"Internet reimbursement"** category, and attach your bill or a screenshot of the charge.
- **Frequency:** Submit monthly — you cannot roll over unused amounts.
- **Pre-approval:** Not required. Just submit and it will be processed with your normal expense cycle.

## Ergonomics

If you experience any discomfort — back pain, wrist strain, eye fatigue — related to your home office setup, don't push through it. **Contact HR** and we'll arrange an ergonomic assessment. Any additional equipment recommended as part of that assessment is covered by the company, separate from your new hire stipend.

<Warning>
  Ergonomic issues tend to get worse if ignored. Reach out to HR as soon as you notice persistent discomfort — early intervention is almost always faster and simpler.
</Warning>

## Co-working spaces

If working from home isn't working for you on a given day (or every day), Autex reimburses up to **$XXX per month** for co-working space memberships or drop-in fees.

- **How to submit:** Use the expense tool and attach a receipt or invoice from the co-working space.
- **Approval:** Manager approval is required before submitting. A quick Slack message to your manager confirming the arrangement is sufficient.

<Tip>
  If you plan to use a co-working space regularly, discuss it with your manager upfront so approval is already in place and you're not chasing it each month.
</Tip>
