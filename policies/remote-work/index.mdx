Autex is a remote-friendly company. Most roles can be performed fully remotely; some require occasional in-person presence for onboarding or team events. Whether you work from a home office, a co-working space, or a combination of both, we want you to have the tools, clarity, and support you need to do great work from wherever you are.

This section covers our expectations for remote employees and the resources available to help you set up a productive, comfortable, and sustainable work environment.

<Note>
  If your role requires occasional in-person attendance, your hiring manager will have communicated this during the offer stage. Reach out to your manager or HR if you have questions about your specific requirements.
</Note>
