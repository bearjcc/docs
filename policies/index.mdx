This section covers the policies that govern how we work together and how the company supports you. Every policy here applies to all full-time and part-time employees unless a specific page notes otherwise. If you have questions about a policy, need to request an exception, or find yourself in a situation that isn't clearly covered, reach out to your manager or the HR team — they're there to help you navigate it.
