**Data Models** define the in-memory shape of **Group** and **Project** snapshots Toolkit reads, compares, exports, and merges. Most features flow through `ProjectData` and `GroupData` rather than calling the DriveWorks SDK ad hoc.

## In this layer

- **Snapshot containers** — `ProjectData`, `GroupData`, and open-session wrappers (`ProjectOpen`, `GroupOpen`).
- **Partial classes** — comparison, merge-delta, and serialization concerns split across `*.Comparison.cs` / `*.MergeDelta.cs` files.
- **Typed sections** — constants, variables, forms, documents, specification flow, and related DTOs hung off the root models.

Boundary: comparison algorithms and TOML IO live in [Core Services](/driveworks/plugins/toolkit/architecture/core-services); scripts consume models through `IAutexScriptContext` in [Plugin Entry](/driveworks/plugins/toolkit/architecture/plugin-entry).
