Security training is mandatory for every Autex employee and contractor. You must complete your initial training within the first 30 days of your start date, and you must complete annual refresher training each time you're notified. This section focuses on the two areas that account for the majority of security incidents at Autex: phishing and data handling. Working through both topics gives you the foundation you need to protect yourself, your colleagues, and the company.

<Note>
  Complete your initial security training within 30 days of your start date. Your manager will be notified if it remains incomplete after that window.
</Note>
