## Sites

Two sites are currently referenced: Hobsonville and Avondale.

## Production Lines

### Hobsonville Line (polyester)

- Insulation
- RAPOS backing for Quietspace Panel, ducting, soffit, and APA

### Needline Line (poly 1, white only)

- Quietspace Panel topping
- Composition backing
- Pavilion Cube felt

### Carpets Line

- Vertiface (lightest to darkest)
- Felt
- Cube (darkest to lightest)
- Symphony backing
- Composition backing
- Quietspace topping (black and grey)

### Special Services Line 1 (flame bonding)

- Composition
- Peel 'n' Stick (CNC)
- Vertiface and felt-covered Quietspace Panel (standard size, Velour)

### Special Services Line 2 (laminator)

- Cube panel
- Quietspace Panel
- Workstation panel
- Symphony
- Visage

### Special Services Line 3 (laminator)

- Ducting
- Soffit (foil and black faced)
- APA (25 mm only)
- Cube (12 mm only, when Line 2 is busy)
- Quietspace Panel (25 mm only, when Line 2 is busy)
