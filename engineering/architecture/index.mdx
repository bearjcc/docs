This section documents the high-level architecture of our systems. It's designed to give you enough context to understand how the major pieces fit together, navigate to the right service when something goes wrong, and make informed decisions when building new features.

For detailed runbooks on specific services, check the service's own README in its repository. If you're not sure where to look or something isn't documented, ask in **#eng** — someone will point you in the right direction.
