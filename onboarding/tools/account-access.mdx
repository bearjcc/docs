Most of your accounts are provisioned automatically the moment HR creates your employee record in the system. You don't need to sign up or submit tickets for the core tools — they're ready and waiting for you when you log in with your company email for the first time. This page explains what you'll have from day one and how to request anything additional.

## Automatically provisioned accounts

The following accounts activate as soon as you sign in with your company email address (`you@autex.com`). No action is required beyond that initial login.

| Tool | Purpose |
|---|---|
| **Company email** | Primary communication — internal messages, calendar invites, and notifications from all connected systems |
| **Slack** | Day-to-day team messaging, direct messages, and channel-based collaboration |
| **Project tracker** | Task management, sprint planning, and issue tracking across teams |
| **HR portal** | Benefits enrollment, time-off requests, payroll information, and employee documents |
| **SSO-easy** | Single sign-on dashboard — your central launchpad for all connected SaaS tools |
| **PasswordBuddy** | Company password manager — stores and autofills credentials for work accounts |

<Tip>
  Start with SSO-easy after logging into your company email. It surfaces all the tools you have access to in one place and is the fastest way to confirm your provisioned accounts are active.
</Tip>

## Request-based access

Some tools require an access request due to cost, data sensitivity, or compliance requirements. Use the table below to understand who approves each type and how to request it.

| Tool / System | How to request | Approval required |
|---|---|---|
| **Infrastructure** (cloud consoles, servers, network tooling) | IT access request at [/it-security/access-requests](/it-security/access-requests) | IT team |
| **Git repositories** | Ask your tech lead to invite you directly | Tech lead |
| **Design platform** | Ask your design lead to add you to the organization | Design lead |
| **Analytics tools** | IT ticket at [/it-security/access-requests](/it-security/access-requests) | Manager approval required |
| **Customer data systems** | IT ticket at [/it-security/access-requests](/it-security/access-requests) | Manager + Security team approval |

<Warning>
  Access to customer data systems is subject to a formal security review. Your manager must submit written justification alongside the request. Plan for a longer review window than standard requests.
</Warning>

## Something missing?

If you need access to a tool that isn't in either table above — or if something you expected to be auto-provisioned hasn't appeared — file an access request ticket at [/it-security/access-requests](/it-security/access-requests).

Include the following in your ticket:

- **The tool or system** you need access to
- **Why you need it** — a brief description of the work it supports
- **Your manager's name** — so IT can route the approval correctly

<Note>
  Routine access requests are fulfilled within **one business day**. Requests requiring manager or security approval may take longer. If your request is time-sensitive, note that in the ticket and follow up directly with your manager to expedite their approval.
</Note>
