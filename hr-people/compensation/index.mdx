Autex's compensation philosophy is straightforward: pay competitively within the market and be transparent about how pay decisions are made. You should understand what factors influence your salary, how your level maps to a pay range, and what can cause your compensation to change over time — without having to guess or rely on hallway conversations.

## What shapes your compensation

Compensation at Autex is built on two foundations: your cash salary, which is tied to your role level and market data, and your equity grant, which gives you a stake in the company's long-term success. Together, these make up your total compensation package.

Pay decisions don't happen in a vacuum. They are connected to your performance. For a full picture of how review outcomes translate into salary changes, see the [Performance Reviews](/hr-people/performance-reviews) page.
