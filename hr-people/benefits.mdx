Autex offers a comprehensive benefits package designed to support your health, financial security, and overall well-being. All full-time employees are eligible for the benefits described on this page starting on **day one** of employment. If you are a part-time employee or contractor, check with HR to confirm which benefits apply to your situation.

## Health insurance

You have three medical plan options, all administered through your medical plan carrier. Choose the plan that best fits your needs and budget during onboarding or annual open enrollment.

<Note>
  You must enroll within **30 days** of your start date. If you miss this window, your next opportunity to enroll is during annual open enrollment, which typically runs in November.
</Note>

## Dental

Dental coverage is available to all full-time employees at **$0/month** for the employee tier. The plan covers two cleanings and sets of X-rays per year at 100%, with no out-of-pocket cost for those preventive visits.

## Vision

Vision coverage is also included at **$0/month** for the employee tier. Your annual eye exam is fully covered, and you receive a **$XXX allowance** each year to put toward glasses or contact lenses.

## 401(k) retirement plan

Autex matches **4% of your salary** up to the IRS annual contribution limit. Vesting is immediate — you own every dollar of the company match from day one.

To enroll or update your contribution rate, log in to the HR portal. Any changes you make take effect in the **next pay period**.

## Enrolling and making changes

You manage all of your benefits elections in the **HR portal**. From there you can:

- Enroll in or waive coverage during your new-hire window
- Review plan documents, carrier contacts, and coverage details
- Update your elections during annual open enrollment (typically November)
- Make mid-year changes if you experience a **qualifying life event** (marriage, birth of a child, loss of other coverage, etc.)

If you have questions about your coverage or need help navigating your options, post in the **#people-help** Slack channel and a member of the People team will get back to you.
