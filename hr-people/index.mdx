The HR & People section is your go-to reference for everything related to your employment at Autex. Whether you're a new hire getting oriented or a long-tenured employee looking up a policy detail, you'll find clear, up-to-date guidance here on the programs and processes that matter most to your day-to-day work life.

For questions that aren't covered in these pages, reach out to the People team directly. The fastest way to get an answer is to post in the **#people-help** Slack channel. You can also submit a formal inquiry or access plan documents through the HR portal.
